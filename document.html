1. Project Mission & Purpose

Bharat Benefits Navigator (BBN) is an AI-driven welfare intelligence platform designed to bridge the gap between government policies and the individual citizen. By leveraging Multi-Agent Orchestration and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), BBN solves the complexity of welfare discovery.

Empowering Discovery

Allowing users to find relevant schemes through natural, conversational language instead of complex forms.

Personalized Eligibility

Translating legal jargon into simple Yes/No matches based on a user's unique socio-economic profile.

3. Key System Definitions

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

A mechanism that retrieves live policy documents from the database and "feeds" them to the AI to prevent hallucinations.

Vector Embedding

Converting human text into 384-dimensional math points using all-MiniLM-L6-v2 to find matches by "meaning" rather than keyword.

Deterministic Logic

Hard-coded rules used by the Eligibility Agent to ensure 100% accuracy in checking age, income, and location criteria.

Socio-Economic Persona

A digital twin of the user containing their state, occupation, and documents used for cross-referencing benefits.
